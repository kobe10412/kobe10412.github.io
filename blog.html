Go Heels!


March 10, 2019





North Carolina Defeats Duke 79-70


The Tarheels have taken down the (highly) overrated Duke Blue Devils in the Dean Smith Center. This is the second time the Heels have defeated Duke this season. With the ACC tournament starting next week, North Carolina is looking at having to potentially embarass them once more once Zion Williamson returns from his injury.
